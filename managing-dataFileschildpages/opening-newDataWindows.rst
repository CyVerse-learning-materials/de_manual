========================
Opening New Data Windows
========================

You can open multiple Data windows, including the ability to open a new window in your home folder as well as in a selected folder location. You can `drag-and-drop <https://wiki.cyverse.org/wiki/display/DEmanual/Moving+a+Data+File+or+Folder>`_ files into a different window.

    1. In the Data window, click **File**.
    2. Either:
        - Click **New Data Window** to open a new window in your home folder.
            *or*
        - Open the target location for the new window and then click **New Data Window at this location** to open the window at a specified location.
