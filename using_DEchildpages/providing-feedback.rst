Providing Feedback and Getting help with the Discovery Environment
------------------------------------------------------------------

.. |helpicon| image:: img/HelpIcon.png

You can submit and contact Support, all within the Discovery Environment. 
Your feedback about the Discovery Environment gives us important information about how your experiences within the DE. 
This includes your ability (or inability) to complete your task, your satisfaction level with the DE, suggestions for features or improvements, and more. 
For feedback on apps within the DE, please see **Using App Ratings and App Comments**


    1. In the DE, click |helpicon| at the top of the screen and then click **feedback**.
    2. In the form, enter your ratings and coments.
    3. When done, click **submit**. You will recieve a follow-up email within seven business days.
